College Achievements

Institutional Recognitions:

  • National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023: PDEU secured a position within the 51-100 rank band in the Innovation category, as recognized by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.
  • Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) Rating: The university was awarded a 4-star rating in the IIC ranking for 2020-21, under the aegis of the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell, Government of India.

Research and Development Initiatives:

  • Solar Panel Manufacturing Center: On January 10th, PDEU inaugurated a state-of-the-art solar panel manufacturing center equipped with a 50 MW production line, marking a significant advancement in renewable energy research and education.
  • Innovation and Incubation Centre (PDEU IIC): Established in 2014, PDEU IIC has supported over 210 startups and secured more than 110 intellectual property rights, focusing on domains such as energy, education, AI, manufacturing, and healthcare.

Faculty and Student Achievements:

  • Faculty Honors: Dr. Debasis Sarkar, Associate Professor in the Civil Engineering Department, was felicitated with the ‘Distinguished Researcher Award’ by Venus International Foundation, Chennai, for his significant contributions in the field of Construction Management in January 2023.
  • Student Competitions: A team of students from the Civil Engineering Department won the DSPS-2023 Student Competition hosted by the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), USA, focusing on data science and the application of Artificial Intelligence in pavement condition monitoring.

International Collaborations:

  • International Exposure Program (IEP): Since 2010, PDEU has facilitated short-term exposure programs with foreign universities, providing students with academic, technical, and cultural experiences to prepare them for dynamic international careers.
